DBZ Encyclopedia

This is a sample Encyclopedia based on the anime Dragonball Z.

DBZ Male characters

Goku

Goku is the main character of the series. He is a saiyan and the son of bardock. He is the strongest character in the series.

Vegeta

Vegeta is the second main character , he is also a saiyan

Gohan

Gohan is the son of Goku and Chi Chi (mother of Gohan) .

Krillin

krillin is the friend of Goku, He is a human. Goku met him on earth.

Master Roshi

Master Roshi is the teacher of both Goku and Krillin. He is the one who trained them inititally with the famous Kame-Hame-Haa .

Picollo

Picollo is also an alien, he is from a planet called namek. He is a firend of Goku. He was birthed by king picollo (his father) in the form of an egg.

Yamcha

He is also one of the fighters who Goku encountered during a tournament. Since then he is a part of the group. He is often mocked to be one of the weakest fighters. Yamcha is also from earth.

Android 17

Android 17 was introduced as a villian in the Dragonball Z series. He was invented by Dr. Gero. He was a strong competitor who was eliminated by Trunks and Vegeta.

Freeza

Freeza was one of the strongest antagonist of the series. He was the main villian who killed krillin and which in return made Goku find out that he is a super saiyan. He was brutally injured by Goku but not killed which made him comeback.

Beerus

Beerus is the god of destruction. He is the one who is capable of destroying anything even universes. lord beerus comes into picture in the Dragon Ball Super series. He is always accompanied with Whis.

King Kai

A comedy lover and brilliant mentor, he acts as Goku's Martial Arts teacher and instructs him in using the Kaio-ken and the Spirit Bomb. He later teaches Krillin, Yamcha, Tien Shinhan, Piccolo, and Chiaotzu and continues to act as a major supporting character throughout the majority of the series.

Whis

Whis is the Guide Angel Attendant of Universe 7's God of Destruction, Beerus, as well as his Martial Arts teacher. Along with the other angels, he is a child of the Grand Minister.

DBZ female characters

Bulma

Bulma is vegeta's wife. She lives on earth. She is the mother of Trunks.
